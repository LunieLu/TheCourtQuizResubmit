The Court Quiz

How to Play:

  • When you click on "Start Quiz", you will be shown a question with 4 possible answers.
  • Please select one answer from the four options.
  • If you get the answer correct, it will turn green.
  • If you get the answer wrong, it will turn red.
  • The quiz will then move onto the next question.
  • There is a maximum of 10 questions and you will receive 100 points for each correct answer.
  • At the end of the quiz, you will be shown your score with the option to enter your name for it to be added to the leaderboard.

Thank you so much for playing "The Court Quiz!"
